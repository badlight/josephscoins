Antoninus Pius

AR Denarius

X
151 - 152 A.D., Rome Mint
3.06g, 17.0mm, 6H
 Obverse: IMP CAES T AEL HADR ANTONINVS AVG PIVS P P, Bust of Antoninus Pius, laureate, facing right
Reverse: TR POT XV COS IIII, Vesta, veiled, draped, standing left, holding simpulum in extended right hand and palladium at shoulder in left

Reference: RIC III 209
