Aurelian

AE Antoninianus

X
Aug/Sep 270 - Oct/Nov 275 A.D., Rome Mint, 10th Officina, 6th Emission
3.985g, 21.0mm, 12H
 Obverse: IMP AVRELIANVS AVG, radiate and cuirassed bust right, from the front
Reverse: ORIENS AVG, Sol standing right, radiate, nude but for cloak over shoulders, raising branch in right, bow in left, left foot on captive

Reference: RIC V 64
