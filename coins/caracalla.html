Caracalla

AR Denarius

205 A.D., Rome Mint
3.40g, 20.0mm, 6H
 Obverse: ANTONINVS PIVS AVG, Bust of Caracalla, laureate, draped, facing right
Reverse: PONTIF TR P VIII COS II, Mars, helmeted, naked except for cloak on left shoulder, standing left, right foot set on helmet, holding branch in right hand and spear in left hand

Reference: RIC IV 80B
