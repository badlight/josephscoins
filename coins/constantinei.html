Constantine I

AE Centenionalis

X
328 A.D., Constantinople Mint, 2nd Officina
3.08g, 19.74mm, 7H
 Obverse: CONSTANTINVS MAX AVG, Bust of Constantine I, rosette-diademed, draped, cuirassed, facing right
Reverse: CONSTANTINIANA DAFNE, Victory, winged, draped, seated left on cippus, head right, holding palm in each hand; before her, trophy; at foot of trophy, kneeling captive, head turned, being spurned by Victory
Exergue: B/-//CONS

Reference: RIC VII Constantinople 35
