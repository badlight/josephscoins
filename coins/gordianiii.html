Gordian III

AR Antoninianus

X
240 A.D., Rome Mint
4.54g, 21.0mm, 6H
 Obverse: IMP CAES M ANT GORDIANVS AVG, Bust of Gordian III, radiate, draped, cuirassed, facing right
Reverse: VIRTVS AVG, Virtus, helmeted, in military attire, standing front, head left, holding branch in right hand and spear in left hand; against right side, shield resting

Reference: RIC IV 39
