Hadrian

AR Denarius

119 - 122 A.D., Rome Mint
3.05g, 19.0mm, 7H
 Obverse: IMP CAESAR TRAIAN HADRIANVS AVG, Bust of Hadrian, laureate, draped, cuirassed, facing right
Reverse: P M TR P COS III SAL AVG, Salus seated left, feeding serpent, who is wrapped around an altar, out of patera with right hand

Reference: RIC II 137C
