Severus Alexander

AE Sestertius

X
231 - 235 A.D., Rome Mint
22.96g, 29.0mm, 12H

Reverse: MARS VLTOR S C, Mars, helmeted, walking right, carrying spear in right hand and shield in left hand

Reference: RIC IV 635
