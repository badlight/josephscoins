Trajan

AR Denarius

X
103 - 111 A.D., Rome Mint
3.15g, 20.0mm, 6H
 Obverse: IMP TRAIANO AVG GER DAC P M TR P, Bust of Trajan, laureate, draped on left shoulder, facing right
Reverse: COS V P P S P Q R OPTIMO PRINC PIET, Pietas, draped, veiled, standing left, with right hand dropping incense on lighted alter left, and holding sceptre in left hand

Reference: RIC II 104
