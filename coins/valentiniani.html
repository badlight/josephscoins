Valentinian I

AE Centenionalis

364 - 367 A.D., Siscia Mint
2.06g, 19.0mm, 6H
 Obverse: D N VALENTINIANVS P F AVG, Bust of Valentinian I, pearl-diademed, draped and cuirassed, facing right
Reverse: SECVRITAS REIPVBLICAE, Victory advancing left, holding wreath and palm

Reference: RIC IX Siscia 7A type II-B
