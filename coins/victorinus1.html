Victorinus

AE Antoninianus

269 - 271 A.D., Cologne Mint
2.65g, 20.0mm, 2H
 Obverse: IMP C VICTORINVS P F AVG, Bust of Victorinus, radiate, cuirassed or draped, facing right
Reverse: PAX AVG, Pax, draped, standing left, holding olive-branch in right hand and sceptre in left hand
Exergue: V in left field, * in right field
Provenance: Ex. South Petherton Hoard

Reference: RIC V 118
